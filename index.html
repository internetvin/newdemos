New Demos is a event series that showcases curated demos in Roncesvalles, Toronto, and it goes like this:

  • 5 mins limit per demo
  • Demos must be prototypes at any stage or creative uses of new technology
  • Attendees vote on the best demo
  • Best demo gets between $1k - $10k prize pool
  • Prize pool comes from tickets purchased by Toronto community
  • Apply to demo: here
