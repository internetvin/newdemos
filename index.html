New Demos is an event series that showcases curated demos in Roncesvalles, Toronto, and it goes like this:

  • 5 mins limit per demo.
  • Demos must be prototypes at any stage or creative uses of new technology.
  • Attendees vote on demos.
  • Demos with most votes gets $1k+ prize pool.
  • Prize pool comes from tickets purchased by Toronto community.
  • All demos are curated, here is criteria.
  • Apply to demo now
